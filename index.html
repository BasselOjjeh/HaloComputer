Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the application?

Go to the courses page and select the course you would like to learn. We recommend starting with the gmail course and working your way up from there!

Why choose Halo Computer?

Halo Computer is based on the idea of learning in a fun and interactive way! Learning how to use a computer can be quite tricky and boring too if not taught in the right way. With the many videos and projects we provide, you will be equipped with the fundamentals to learning how to use a computer!

Who is Halo Computer for?

Anyone can use Halo Computer, however our courses are more oriented towards beginners in computer usage. Also our multiple language availability is put in place in order to cater audiences from different countries especially less economicaly developed countries.

How can you contact us?

Don't worry! We have a contact page on the top right of the navigation bar. Go there and type in a message or ask any question you like.
